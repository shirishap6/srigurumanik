Shri ManikPrabhu Jayanthi Utsavam

Shri Manik Prabhu Jayanti Utshav is celebrated for 5 to 6 days. It starts on Margashira Shudda Ekadashi and ends with Darbar.

Shri Manik Prabhu Punya Tithi:

Shri Manik Prabhu Punya Tithi is celebrated on Margashira Shudda Ekadashi at Prabhu Nagar.

Shri Manik Prabhu Jayanthi:
Shri Manik Prabhu Jayanthi and Datta Jayanthi is also celebrated on Margashira Pournami at Prabhu Nagar.
Darbar:
The utshav ends with Darbar, which is celebrated at night time on Margasshira Bahula Padyami.
