Ananthagiri Pradakshina

Ananthagiri Hills is located in Vikarabad District, Telangana.

The Anathagiri Pradakshina is organised on 5th jan 2022 by Manik Prabhu Samsthan Prabhu Nagar and Hindu Jana Shakti. The spiritual walk started at 6'o clock in the morning from Rajiv Nagar Bhavani Maata Temple and reached Anantha Padmanaba Swamy Temple through Bandabavi Hanuman Devasthanam, Godhumaguda, Kerelli and Bugga Rama Lingeswara Swamy Devasthanam.
